Frequently Asked Questions

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes! All plans can be cancelled at any time with no penalties. Monthly plans require 30 days notice.

Do you offer student discounts?

Yes! Students with valid ID receive 20% off all plans. Contact us for details.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, debit cards, and bank transfers. Auto-pay is available.

Is there a joining fee?

No joining fees! We believe in making fitness accessible to everyone.

Can I freeze my membership?

Yes, you can freeze your membership for up to 3 months per year for medical or travel reasons.

Do you offer corporate memberships?

Yes! We offer special rates for companies with 10+ employees. Contact us for a custom quote.
